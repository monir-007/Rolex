ROLEX History

The history of Rolex is inextricably linked to the visionary spirit of Hans Wilsdorf, its founder.

rolex made in switzerland

The history of Rolex and Swiss watchmaking are inextricably linked. Pioneer in the development of the wristwatch, the brand is at the origin of numerous major watchmaking innovations, and has registered over 500 patents in the course of its history.

the watchemakers

Experts dressed in white, at computerized workstations, displaying absolute concentration in silence and calm – the atmosphere in the Rolex workshops is one of deepest serenity and complete devotion

the art of the sheen

It takes several years for a polisher – nowadays known as a termineur, a finisher – to reach such a level of proficiency and assurance. A three-year apprentice ship to learn the trade, its principles, tools, materials, the well-defined techniques and processes in force at Rolex

dials - rolex watch making

The dial gives the watch its face and an identity of its own. It also displays the functions – hours, minutes, seconds, date and other indicators of time. Interface between the wearer and the watch’s mechanical movement

Bracelets and Clasps

Bracelets and clasps play a full part in the unique pleasure of wearing a Rolex watch. Their ergonomics are carefully studied, while their robustness and reliability, their aesthetics and lustre are all inherent to the personality of the timepiece.
